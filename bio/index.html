Breed Profile

Anna Skaya is a rare breed: part scientist, part builder, part feral startup founder. She created Basepaws—the first direct-to-consumer cat genetics company—because she believed that if humans could decode their own DNA, cats deserved the same.

Like any good cat, Anna pursued the idea with single-minded intensity. She pounced on an untapped market, hunted down venture funding, and groomed a scrappy startup into a company that Zoetis—the world's largest animal health company—ultimately acquired.

Before Basepaws, she roamed across biotech, data science, and product design, collecting skills the way cats collect sunny spots. Her signature move? Blending deep technical rigor with obsessive consumer empathy. She builds things that are both scientifically sound and irresistibly useful.

Nine Lives

Every good cat gets more than one shot. Here are hers so far.

Life 1

The Scientist

Cut her teeth in biotech and data science, developing an instinct for turning complex data into things people can actually use.

Life 2

The Designer

Learned that the prettiest data in the world means nothing if the product feels bad. Became obsessed with consumer experience.

Life 3

The Cat Whisperer

Asked the question nobody else was asking: "Why can I sequence my own genome but not my cat's?" Founded Basepaws in 2016.

Life 4

The Kickstarter

Launched an audacious crowdfunding campaign that captured global attention and proved there was a massive, untapped market of curious cat parents.

Life 5

The Fundraiser

Secured venture capital and assembled a team of geneticists, engineers, and cat lovers. Built the world's largest feline genomics database.

Life 6

The Operator

Scaled Basepaws from scrappy startup to a real company—DTC sales, lab operations, breed and health reports, a growing community of devoted cat people.

Life 7

The Exit

Basepaws was acquired by Zoetis, the world's largest animal health company. A landmark moment in pet-tech and a validation of the vision.

Life 8

The Builder

Turned her attention to AI, proptech, and connected living—building smart spaces and platforms that blend technology with warmth.

Life 9
