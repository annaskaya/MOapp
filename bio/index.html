Anna Skaya

Building at the intersection of science, technology, and design—from pet genomics to smart property platforms.

Anna Skaya is a serial entrepreneur and technologist who founded Basepaws, the first direct-to-consumer cat genetics company, transforming how millions of pet owners understand their animals' health and heritage.

Under Anna's leadership, Basepaws pioneered at-home DNA and health screening kits for cats—bringing the same revolution that personal genomics created for human health into the veterinary world. The company was acquired by Zoetis, the world's largest animal health company, validating a bold thesis: that personalized, data-driven pet care is the future.

Before Basepaws, Anna honed her craft across biotech, data science, and product design. Her approach blends deep technical rigor with an eye for consumer experience—a combination that has become her signature across every venture.
