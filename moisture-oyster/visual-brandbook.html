Always

Do This

  • Shoot in warm, cinematic low light
  • Show real skin — freckles, pores, glow
  • Use iridescent color shifts in every gradient
  • Frame product next to skincare, never medicine
  • Favor extreme close-ups over wide shots
  • Let massive negative space breathe
  • Dark backgrounds make iridescent colors pop
  • Oversized type for impact, light weight for body
  • Crop boldly — show fragments, create curiosity

Never

Not This

  • Flat white pharmacy lighting
  • Airbrush skin to plastic perfection
  • Clinical or medical imagery of any kind
  • Product on white void background
  • Stock photography of “happy people”
  • Hard drop shadows or bevels
  • Anything that looks like a Systane ad
  • Pastels without depth — flat is dead
  • Safe, expected, forgettable layouts
